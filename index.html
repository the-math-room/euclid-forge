Euclid Forge Click empty canvas: add point Drag point: move point Drag triangle/circle: move free source points Shift-click: select point/segment/circle/triangle J: join 2/3 selected free points into segment/triangle C: circle from center/through points G: centroid of selected triangle M: side midpoints of selected triangle Delete/Backspace: delete selected nodes H: hide selected nodes U: unhide all hidden nodes PageUp/PageDown: move selected forward/backward Shift+PageUp/PageDown: move selected to front/back Arrow keys: pan viewport + / -: zoom viewport [ / ]: rotate viewport \: reset viewport rotation 0: reset viewport Ctrl/Cmd+S: save workspace Ctrl/Cmd+O: open workspace Ctrl/Cmd+Z: undo Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+Z or Ctrl/Cmd+Y: redo
